### About

I lead [SAMYORA PRIVATE LIMITED](https://www.samyora.co), a technology company delivering end-to-end digital solutions for startups and enterprises. We specialize in building scalable web applications, cross-platform mobile apps, and AI-powered systems that drive measurable business outcomes.

**Current Focus:**
- Architecting production-grade applications using Next.js, React, and Node.js
- Developing AI/ML solutions with TensorFlow and Python
- Building Web3 applications with Solidity and Ethereum
- Leading a distributed engineering team across multiple time zones

**Background:**
- Computer Science, Central University of Rajasthan (2026)
- 1st Place, AnalytiX 2025 DataOps Showdown - Financial Risk Classification using STOA-Attention LSTM (98.8% accuracy)
- Delivered solutions for Playtics, Dusker AI, BuildVerse, and other clients
